## ☕ 1. Cafe Marketing Assistant (Local Business Use Case)

### 🎯 Goal
Help cafes attract more tourists using AI-generated content.

### 🧠 Prompt
```text
You are a digital marketing expert.
Create an Instagram caption for a rooftop cafe in Pushkar.
Highlight sunset views, include emojis, and add a call-to-action.




✅ Output
Engaging caption with emojis
Tourist-focused messaging
CTA like “Visit us today!”
💡 Impact
Increased Instagram engagement
Better tourist footfall
Improved brand visibility
